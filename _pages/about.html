Researcher

In 2013, I graduated from the I.S.I.B. in Brussels with a degree in software engineering, and my first job was a two-year R&D project in the area of network sonification using Laras. The major objective was to produce sounds from network packets so that the network infrastructure could be directly heard through audio.

Client Solution Engineer

I began working with Odoo as a client solution developer in September 2015, where my duties included creating and delivering customized solutions for the company's clients. I acquired the skills necessary to create what is truly important for the client. The ability to deliver high-quality services quickly is what defines a service's excellence, and I firmly believe that business analysis and client management are crucial to achieving this.

Head of Development

I joined Skipr as a software engineer in September 2017 in order to create an app that would allow everyone to move anywhere. We created a product from scratch while validating every step along the way. In two years, we went from 1 to 40 members, and we are now based in Belgium and prepared to expand throughout Europe. My responsibility was to aid our development team in achieving its objectives in all areas, including processes, code quality, architecture, and personnel management.
